Information

This site gives waitresses, waiters and cooks the opportunity to communicate better with each other and achieve better service.

Choosing the "waiter " option will provide you with the ability to take and alter orders, taking them, serving them and deleting them. Also you will gain full control over the menu.
Choosing "Cook " will provide you with the ability to hand the meal to the waiters and let them know when one`s meal is ready.
